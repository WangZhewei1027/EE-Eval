\caption{Box plot comparison of FSM similarity scores across six AI models (N=687 total samples). Each box represents the interquartile range (IQR) from the first quartile (Q1) to the third quartile (Q3), with the horizontal line indicating the median. Whiskers extend to the minimum and maximum values within the data range. \textbf{Key findings:} (1) GPT-5-Mini achieves the highest median score (79.11\%) with relatively low variance (StdDev=6.46\%); (2) GPT-3.5-Turbo and DeepSeek-Chat show similar median performance ($\sim$70\%) but different variance patterns; (3) Qwen-1.5-0.5B demonstrates the lowest performance (median=45.00\%) with moderate variance; (4) ANOVA analysis confirms statistically significant inter-model differences (F=120.27, $p<0.001$). Color scheme: Blue (GPT-5-Mini), Red (GPT-3.5-Turbo), Green (DeepSeek-Chat), Orange (GPT-4o-Mini), Purple (Llama-3.2-1B), Gray (Qwen-1.5-0.5B).}
