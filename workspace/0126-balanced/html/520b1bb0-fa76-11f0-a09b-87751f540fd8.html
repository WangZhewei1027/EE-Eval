Software Development Life Cycle

Phase 1: Requirements Gathering Gathering requirements, defining the problem, and identifying the solution.

Phase 2: Design Creating a design, developing a prototype, and testing the solution.

Phase 3: Implementation Writing the code, integrating the solution, and testing the final product.

Phase 4: Testing Testing the solution, identifying bugs, and making necessary improvements.

Phase 5: Deployment Deploying the solution, making it available to the public, and monitoring its performance.

Phase 6: Maintenance Updating and improving the solution, fixing bugs, and ensuring its continued success.
