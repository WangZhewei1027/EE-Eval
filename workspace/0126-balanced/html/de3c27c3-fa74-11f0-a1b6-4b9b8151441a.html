What is Topological Sort?

Topological sort is an ordering of vertices in a directed acyclic graph (DAG) where for every directed edge u → v, vertex u comes before v in the ordering.

It's commonly used in:

  • Task scheduling (determining order of tasks with dependencies)
  • Build systems (determining compilation order)
  • Course prerequisites
  • Event scheduling

The algorithm works by repeatedly finding nodes with no incoming edges (no dependencies), adding them to the result, and removing their outgoing edges.
