Overfitting (Interactive Demo)

This interactive visualization shows how polynomial models can underfit, fit well, or overfit data. Generate noisy samples from a fixed "true" function, fit a polynomial of adjustable degree, and compare training vs test error. Increase degree too far and the model will follow noise — that is overfitting.
