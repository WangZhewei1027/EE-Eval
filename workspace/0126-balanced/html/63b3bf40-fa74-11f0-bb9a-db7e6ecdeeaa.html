Agile Methodology Demonstration

Agile Methodology is an iterative approach to software development and project management that emphasizes flexibility, collaboration, continuous improvement, and delivering small, functional increments of a product frequently.

This interactive board simulates a common Agile workflow with columns for Backlog, In Progress, and Done. You can drag tasks across columns to simulate work progression during a sprint.

How this demo relates to Agile: The tasks represent user stories / features that move through the sprint workflow - from backlog, to active development, to done. Agile encourages working in small increments, frequent delivery, and adapting as progress happens. Drag tasks to simulate that workflow.
