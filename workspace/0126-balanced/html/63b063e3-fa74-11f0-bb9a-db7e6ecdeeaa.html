AVL Tree Visualization & Demo

AVL Tree Basics:
- An AVL Tree is a self-balancing binary search tree.
- Each node maintains a balance factor = height(left subtree) - height(right subtree).
- Valid balance factors are -1, 0, or 1.
- Rotations (single and double) are used to restore balance after insertions or removals.

Controls: Input an integer and click Insert or Remove.
Visualization: Nodes are shown as circles with their values.
Red edges & nodes highlight rotations and rebalancing actions.
