Monitor (Concurrency) — Bounded Buffer Demo

This page demonstrates the "Monitor" concurrency abstraction: a mutual exclusion lock combined with condition variables (wait/notify). The demo runs multiple producers and consumers operating on a bounded buffer protected by a Monitor implementation. The Monitor ensures only one thread/method executes its critical section at a time and enables threads to wait for conditions (e.g. "notFull"/"notEmpty").

Notes:
  • This implementation models a "Mesa-style" monitor: notify wakes a waiting thread but does not transfer the lock directly. A notified thread re-enters the entry queue and must re-acquire the lock.
  • The Monitor class implemented here offers simple enter/exit (mutex) and wait/notify(condition) primitives built on Promises.
