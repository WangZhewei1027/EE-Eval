Priority Queue (Binary Heap) Interactive Demonstration

This page demonstrates a Priority Queue implemented with a binary heap. You can add items with a priority, remove the top item, change priorities, and watch how the heap restructures itself. Choose Min-heap (lowest priority = highest importance) or Max-heap.
