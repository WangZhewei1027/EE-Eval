- Sender increases cwnd exponentially (Slow Start) until cwnd >= ssthresh.
- After that it increases linearly (Congestion Avoidance).
- On packet loss: timeout reduces cwnd to 1 and ssthresh = cwnd/2 (Slow Start restart).
- 3 duplicate ACKs can trigger Fast Retransmit/Fast Recovery to avoid slow start.
Try changing bandwidth, buffer and loss to see how cwnd reacts.
Interactive simulation — educational only. This model is simplified to illustrate the dynamics of TCP-like congestion control (Slow Start, Congestion Avoidance, Timeout, Fast Retransmit/Recovery).
