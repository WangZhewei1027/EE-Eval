Understanding the Deque

👥

Double-Ended

A deque allows insertion and removal of elements from both the front and rear, making it more versatile than a simple queue.

⚡

Constant Time Operations

All basic operations (add/remove front/rear) can be performed in O(1) time with proper implementation.

🔀

Flexible Usage

Can be used as both a stack (LIFO) and queue (FIFO), or a combination of both.
