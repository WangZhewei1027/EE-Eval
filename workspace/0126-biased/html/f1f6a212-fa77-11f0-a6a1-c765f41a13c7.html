Recursion
A visual exploration of self-similarity — smaller copies arise within larger forms, rotating and breathing in harmonious sequence.

What you're observing

Each circle spawns a ring of smaller circles around its edge. The process repeats for a few generations — a core idea of recursion: a function that calls itself to build complexity from simple rules. Subtle motion and layered glows emphasize depth and repetition.

Depth: 4 • Branches per node: 4 • Palette: dynamic

Controls

Two minimal controls — observe the geometry, then gently nudge the composition.

Design notes

The visualization uses recursive geometry rendered in SVG. Each depth level gets an alternating rotational animation, creating a hypnotic interplay of motion. Color palettes are curated for contrast and elegance; glassmorphism and ambient glow enhance perceived depth.
