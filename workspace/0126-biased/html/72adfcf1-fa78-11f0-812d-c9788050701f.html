Digital Signatures

A cryptographic mechanism for verifying the authenticity and integrity of digital messages or documents.

1

Create Signature

Sender creates a unique digital signature using their private key

2

Attach to Document

The signature is attached to the original document

3

Verification

Recipient verifies using sender's public key
