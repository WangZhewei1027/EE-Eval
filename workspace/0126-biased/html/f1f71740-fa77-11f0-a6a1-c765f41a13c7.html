Big-Theta (Θ) Notation
A concise way to describe asymptotic growth
Big-Theta notation formalizes when two functions grow at the same rate asymptotically. If f(n) is Θ(g(n)), then for sufficiently large n, f(n) is sandwiched between c₁·g(n) and c₂·g(n) for positive constants c₁ and c₂.
