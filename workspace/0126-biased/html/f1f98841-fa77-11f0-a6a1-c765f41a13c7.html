What you’re seeing

This is a stylized feed-forward neural network: nodes are organized into layers and connected by weighted links. The brighter pulses represent information (activations) propagating through the network from left to right. In real neural networks, weights and activations are numeric; here, motion and light communicate flow, depth, and connectivity with visual elegance.
