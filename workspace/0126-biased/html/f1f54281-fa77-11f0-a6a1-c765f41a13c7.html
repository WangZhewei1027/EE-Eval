Priority Queue

A visual and aesthetic demonstration of enqueuing and dequeuing by priority

A priority queue organizes items so that the element with the highest priority can be extracted first. This demo shows a max-heap implementation: new items are enqueued and bubble up based on priority; when dequeued the top item is removed and the heap rearranges itself. Watch the animated nodes flow into the heap and observe swaps as the structure maintains its invariants.
