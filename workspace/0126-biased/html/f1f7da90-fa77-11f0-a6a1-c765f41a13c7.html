What is Indexing?

Indexing organizes content so queries can find relevant documents instantly. Terms (words, tokens) are mapped to posting lists — compact references to documents. This view shows two metaphors: a tactile stack of index cards and an inverted index that points terms to documents. The animated highlights represent tokens being registered into the index.

Minimal controls: use "Play" to pause/resume the visual flow, and "Explain" to toggle this overlay. The visualization emphasizes clarity and aesthetics over interaction.
