K-Nearest Neighbors

The KNN algorithm is a simple, intuitive machine learning method that classifies new data points based on the majority class among their K closest neighbors in the feature space.

In this visualization, blue and red points represent two different classes. When you add a new green point, the algorithm finds its 3 nearest neighbors (connected by lines) and classifies it based on their majority.
