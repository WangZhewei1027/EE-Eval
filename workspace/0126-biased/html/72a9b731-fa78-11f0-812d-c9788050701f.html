About Insertion Sort

Insertion sort is a simple sorting algorithm that builds the final sorted array one item at a time. It works by repeatedly taking the next element and inserting it into its correct position within the already sorted portion. Though not the most efficient for large datasets, its simplicity and elegance make it beautiful to visualize and efficient for small or nearly sorted data.
