What is Two Pointers?
Two pointers is an efficient technique for problems on sorted arrays where two indices move towards each other to satisfy conditions (e.g. finding pairs with a target sum).
Visualization Notes
Watch the left (L) and right (R) indices evaluate sums. The animation highlights comparisons and shows why movement depends on the sum vs target.
