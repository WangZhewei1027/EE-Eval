What is NoSQL?

NoSQL stands for "Not Only SQL," and it refers to a family of databases that don't use the traditional relational model of SQL. Instead, they use a variety of data models and storage systems.

Some common characteristics of NoSQL databases include:

  • Document-oriented data model
  • Key-value pairs
  • Document-based storage
  • Flexible schema
  • Scalability
