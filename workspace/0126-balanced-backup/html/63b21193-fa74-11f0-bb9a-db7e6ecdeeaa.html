Sliding Window Technique Visualization

The Sliding Window technique is a method for efficiently solving problems that involve arrays or sequences. It involves creating a "window" (a subset of elements) which slides over the data structure to examine a range of elements without having to repeatedly recalculate everything.

Here we demonstrate finding the maximum sum of any contiguous subarray of size k using a sliding window.
