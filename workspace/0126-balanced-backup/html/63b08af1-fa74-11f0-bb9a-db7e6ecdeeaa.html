About B-Tree:
A B-tree of order t is a self-balancing tree data structure that maintains sorted data and allows searches, sequential access, insertions, and deletions in logarithmic time.

Nodes: Each node can have between t-1 and 2t - 1 keys.
Children: Each internal node can have from t to 2t children.
Root: The root may have fewer than t-1 keys.
