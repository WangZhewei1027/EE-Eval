This diagram illustrates the concept of deadlock through processes (blue circles) and resources (pink circles). Solid arrows represent resources held by processes; dashed arrows represent resources requested by processes.

When each process waits for a resource held by another in a circular chain, no process can proceed — a deadlock occurs.
