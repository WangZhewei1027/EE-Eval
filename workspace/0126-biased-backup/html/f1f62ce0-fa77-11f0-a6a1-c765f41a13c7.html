Ternary Search

A refined, animated demonstration of dividing to conquer

Ternary Search is a divide-and-conquer algorithm that splits a sorted range into three parts, checks two middle points, and discards one third each iteration. This visualization shows the elegant reduction of the search interval until the target is found (or not).
