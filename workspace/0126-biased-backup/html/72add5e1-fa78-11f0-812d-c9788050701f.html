AES (Advanced Encryption Standard)

The gold standard in symmetric encryption, using 128, 192 or 256-bit keys. Adopted by the U.S. government and used worldwide.

Block Cipher Key sizes: 128/192/256-bit

ChaCha20

A modern stream cipher designed for high performance in software implementations, often used as an alternative to AES.

Stream Cipher Key size: 256-bit

Twofish

A symmetric key block cipher with a block size of 128 bits and key sizes up to 256 bits, known for its flexibility.

Block Cipher Key sizes: 128/192/256-bit
