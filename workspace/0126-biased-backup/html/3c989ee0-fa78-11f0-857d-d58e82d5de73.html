Two Pointers Technique
The Two Pointers technique is an elegant and efficient way to solve array and string problems by using two indices moving from different ends toward each other (or in the same direction). This approach often reduces the time complexity drastically by avoiding nested loops. Observe how two pointers, left and right, move inward to find pairs or inspect elements visually.
