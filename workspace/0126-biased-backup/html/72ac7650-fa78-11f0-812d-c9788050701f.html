Encrypted Connection
🔐

Encryption

HTTPS uses TLS/SSL encryption to protect all communication between your browser and the website. This prevents eavesdroppers from reading sensitive information.

📜

Data Integrity

HTTPS ensures that data cannot be modified or corrupted during transfer without being detected. This protects against man-in-the-middle attacks.

✅

Authentication

HTTPS verifies that you're communicating with the intended website and not an imposter. This is done through SSL certificates issued by trusted authorities.

HTTPS is the foundation of a secure internet. Always look for the lock icon 🔒 in your browser's address bar.
