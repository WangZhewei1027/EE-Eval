Support Vector Machine Interactive Explorer

This demonstration lets you:

  • Generate and manipulate datasets (2D points) from various classes.
  • Train SVM classifiers with different kernels, parameters, and hyperparameter tuning.
  • Visualize the data points, decision boundary, margins, and support vectors.
  • Manually add, move, or remove points to explore the effects on the SVM.
  • Examine hinge loss and margins per point.
  • Perform batch training, save/load datasets and models in JSON.
  • Explore multi-class classification via one-vs-one or one-vs-rest approaches.
  • Compare custom linear solver and simplified SMO solver implementations.

Visualization & Interaction

Use mouse interaction in canvas:

  • Left Click: Add points (if Add Point Mode ON) or Select nearest point.
  • Drag Selected Point: Move its position and retrain SVM automatically.
  • Delete Selected Point: Press Del key to remove selected point and retrain.
  • Mouse Wheel: Zoom in/out centered on mouse pointer.
  • Right Click + Drag: Pan canvas.
