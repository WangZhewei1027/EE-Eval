HTTPS

Secure by Design

HTTPS (HyperText Transfer Protocol Secure) encrypts communication between your browser and websites, protecting your data from eavesdroppers and tampering. It ensures privacy, integrity, and authentication, making our online world safer.

Why HTTPS Matters

Without HTTPS, data sent over the internet is vulnerable to interception and modification, risking your privacy and security. HTTPS uses SSL/TLS encryption to create a secure channel, verifying website identities and keeping your information safe even on untrusted networks.
