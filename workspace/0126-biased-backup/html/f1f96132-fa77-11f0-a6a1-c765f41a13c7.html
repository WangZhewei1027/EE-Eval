Support Vector Machine

SVMs find the maximum-margin hyperplane that separates classes. This visualization illustrates a simple linear SVM: watch the hyperplane move, the margin expand, and the support vectors emerge — the critical points that define the decision boundary.

What you are seeing

Two classes are plotted in a plane. The SVM seeks a hyperplane (the colorful line) that maximizes the margin — the band between the dashed lines. The points that touch the margin are the support vectors: they uniquely determine the solution. This is a stylized, visual walkthrough of how SVMs form robust decision boundaries.
